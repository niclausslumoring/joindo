About Us

Welcome to PT. Joindo Eka Handal, your trusted partner in innovation and education. Established in 2016, we initially began our journey by distributing cutting-edge electronic products, including smartphone and accessories

In 2017 , we recognized the transformative power of education and redirected our focus towards providing innovative educational technology and STEAM (Science, Technology , Engineering, Art and Mathematics ) products.

At PT.Joindo Eka Handal we are dedicated to shaping the future of learning through our commitment to excellence and passion for educational advancement.

UNICAMP

PT.Joindo Eka Handal took a significant step by launching the inaugural UNICAMP (Teachers Development Bootcamp), a pioneering STEAM Teachers training camp in Indonesia. Over two nights and three days , we welcomed over 40 educators, providing them with an immersive experience in the latest educational technology and curriculum.

TETRA & CYBERSPACE

Building on the success of the first UNICAMP, we expanded our impact in 2023 by hosting the second edition. This time , more than 60 educators from various cities across Indonesia joined us  for an enriching and collaborative learning experience. At PT. Joindo Eka Handal, we remain committed to empowering educators and advancing STEAM Education through initiatives like UNICAMP.

In response to our continued growth, PT.Joindo Eka Handal strategically expanded its footprint by establishing two subsidiaries. PT.Taman Teknologi Nusantara (TETRA) was created with dedicated focus on distribution and teachers training, ensuring a comprehensive approach to delivering our innovative educational solutions.

Simultaneously, PT.Lab Inovasi Teknologi (Cyberspace) emerged as a subsidiary with a distinct mission - to serve as a Technology Education Course Center and provider of extracurricular program activities for schools.
